## Overview

**KELONG (Web)** is the web app version of KELONG, a water quality monitoring system built as a preventive measure against early fish kills in floating fish farms. It was developed as a side project for one of my courses, adapting the original mobile app into a browser-based experience with **ReactJS**.

Paired with a network of water quality sensors and a deep learning forecasting model, the system predicts upcoming water quality conditions and flags risks that could be detrimental to fish health — giving fish farm operators time to act before conditions become critical. Operators can also control an aeration system remotely to help stabilize water quality in response to forecasted risks.

**Live Demo:** [aquaculture-web.vercel.app](https://aquaculture-web.vercel.app/)

## Main Features

- 📊 **Dashboard** - real-time and forecasted water quality parameters at a glance
- 🔔 **Notifications** - early-warning alerts when forecasts indicate risk to fish health
- 💨 **Aerator Control** - remote control of aeration hardware to respond to water quality risks
- 📥 **CSV Import** - upload water quality data in bulk via CSV
- 📤 **CSV Export** - download recorded/forecasted data as CSV for offline analysis
